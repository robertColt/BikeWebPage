Salsa BUCKSAW

OFF-ROAD EXPLORATION / RUGGED TRAIL / BACKCOUNTRY RIDING

Bucksaw is our paradigm-shattering full-suspension fatbike, blurring the lines between “fatbike” and “full-suspension mountain bike.” Whether tackling the rocky ledges of the deserts southwest or the bumpy Icelandic tundra, Bucksaw eats it all up in confidence. The “Where can I ride Bucksaw?” question in your mind will quickly change to “Where can’t I ride Bucksaw?”

Pros: Rides over anything with ease. Suspension is dialed and overall weight is pretty reasonable. Tires are great in summer conditions and the Reverb post makes this a fun bike on the descents.

Recommendation: The Bucksaw is the only mountain bike many riders will need. Handles like a mountain bike with super powers.
